# ADR-0001: Short imperative title

## Context

What is the forcing function? Cite specific code / governance / market conditions.
Avoid hand-waving ("we want clean code"). 3-5 sentences.

## Decision

The concrete choice being made. Imperative ("Use X", not "We will use X").

## Consequences

### Positive

- Specific, falsifiable benefit
- Another specific benefit

### Negative

- Specific cost / trade-off
- Another specific cost

### Neutral

- Behavioural change that's neither good nor bad

## Alternatives considered

- **Alternative A** — rejected because <reason>
- **Alternative B** — rejected because <reason>
